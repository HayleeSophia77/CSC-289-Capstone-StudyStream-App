# CSC-289-Capstone-StudyStream-App

## StudyStream – Student Task Management System

StudyStream is a task management system built for college students who are juggling way too much at once.

Instead of bouncing between a bunch of apps just to figure out what’s due, StudyStream puts everything in one place so you can actually stay organized without overthinking it.

---

## The Problem

College isn’t hard because the work is impossible. It’s hard because everything is scattered everywhere.

- Canvas  
- Emails  
- Group chats  
- Random Google Docs  
- Your own notes  

And somehow you’re expected to keep track of all of it.

That’s how people:
- Miss assignments  
- Get screwed in group projects  
- Realize too late that everything is due at the same time  

---

## The Idea

StudyStream is basically your academic command center.

Instead of guessing what you’re forgetting, you can:
- See everything in one place  
- Know what to work on next  
- Actually stay ahead instead of constantly catching up  

---

## What It Does

### Keep Track of Classes
- All assignments in one place  
- Due dates and priorities  
- Break big projects into smaller steps  
- Schedule actual time to work on things  

### Group Project Support (Future Development)
- Planned feature for shared task tracking  
- Will allow everyone to see what they’re responsible for  
- Aims to reduce miscommunication in group projects  
- Goal is to track progress without constantly reminding people  

### Help You Stay Ahead
- Warns you when your week is about to be overwhelming  
- Shows how heavy your workload actually is  
- Helps you plan before it’s too late  

---

## How It Works

StudyStream runs as a web or mobile app, so you can:
- Add and manage assignments anytime  
- Keep everything in one place  
- Access your schedule wherever you are  

---

## Who It’s For

- Students balancing school, work, and life  
- People who feel like they’re always forgetting something  
- Anyone tired of checking multiple platforms  

---

## Why This Is Different

Most tools don’t actually fix the problem:

- Everything is spread across different platforms  
- You have to check things manually  
- Nothing shows your full workload clearly  

StudyStream focuses on:
- simplicity with everything in one place  
- clarity so you know what to do next  
- actual support instead of just listing due dates  

---

## Goal

Make school feel manageable again instead of overwhelming.

---

## Why This Exists

This idea came from a real problem. You can be a good student and still fall behind just because everything is unorganized and spread out.

So instead of another app that just adds more to manage, this is meant to be something that actually helps you keep your life together.
